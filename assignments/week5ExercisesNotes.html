Messing with Errors

Use the console for this page by pressing F12 on the keyboard.

Enter the following functions in the console.


getAccount()

- entering a, b, c, or d in the prompt will return info.

- entering something other than those will trigger the error message.


getFunds()

- entering a, b, or c will return the account name and balance.

- entering d will log a warning about the account being overdrawn along with the name and balance.

- entering something other than those will trigger the error message.
